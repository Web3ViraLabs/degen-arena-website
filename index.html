So, What is

Double Jump?

Double Jump is a new fast-paced, action-packed platformer game that is pushing the boundaries of what is possible in the world of browser and mobile gaming. With a unique blend of intuitive controls and challenging obstacles, Double Jump is the perfect game for players of all ages who are looking for a thrilling and immersive gaming experience.

Grab your keyboard and get ready to take the leap â Double Jump is waiting for you! To learn more, check out our FAQ below or join our Discord Community for future updates.

What is the gameplay of Double Jump?

Double Jump is a fast and frenetic race-to-finish platform-royale game where players can compete with their friends or anyone across the world to win. Our races take place on levels that vary from a floating medieval castle to the jungle ruins of an ancient civilization. Each level is unique and contains colorful but tricky obstacles on top of fierce online opponents.

Our game only requires players to do three things: run, jump and hit your opponents! Depending on the map, there are two victory conditions: be the first to finish or be the last player standing. Don't be afraid to use shortcuts, and of course remember to double jump!

Where can I play Double Jump?

Double Jump is a web-browser based game that will eventually be also released on mobile. Our current game build can be played directly on our partner platform Magic Eden. We eventually plan to launch our full game on both Magic Eden as well as our own domain.

What are Jumpers?

Jumpers are our signature Double Jump character that are your ticket to play the full game when launched! They range from common Jumpers, which can be minted on our website closer to launch, to Limited-Edition Jumpers, which will only be dropped through special events. Each Jumper rewards daily $JUMP tokens when staked, allowing users to participate in the game ecosystem daily. We have already sold out our first collection of Origin Collection Jumpers, which are available on secondary markets like Magic Eden.Holding any Jumper will allow you to play the full game when launched!

What new features will be in the full game launch?

Our vertical slice build on Magic Eden showcases two (2) game modes (Party Mode and Free Play mode), two (2) game types (Race-to-Finish and Survival) and three (3) characters. Our Full Game Launch will utilize a Freemium-based model to dramatically expand on our existing features to six (6) game modes, six (6) game types, and many more skins. Weâll also continue to design new game modes and game types based on user feedback for future seasons.Example game modes include Token Tournaments, where players will be able to enter tournaments with $JUMP for a chance to win bigger prize pools, as well as Time Challenges for players that look to speed run. Game types will include Capture the Flag, Coin Collecting and Relay Races.
